Boxing/Kicking Boxing:

Offering Boxing as a Cross-Training Method for anyone who is interested in changing up their regular workout routine. Boxing is an effective way to lose weight, build cardio endurance, build hand speed and power, relieve stress and suitable for anyone of all ages.

Cardio Endurance:

Total body workouts in order to build Cardio Endurance, increase metabolism and assist in the recovery of a heavy lifting session. Cardio Classes are a perfect fit for anyone’s workout routine and blends perfectly with a Boxing Routine. If weight loss and muscle tone is a personal goal that you have in mind, then a combination of Boxing and Cardio would be a suitable program for you.

Functional Movement Training:

Training your body to work efficiently during daily activities. Functional Movement Training Specializes in working every muscle during a workout specializing in Mobility, increasing Range of Motion and increasing overall Core Strength and Stability.

Strength and Conditioning:

Conditioning the body to increase mobility, proper movement, posture power and overall strength. These are a few important aspects of Strength and Conditioning. However, there is a significant value to Strength and Conditioning which does not only include one’s ability to lift as much weight as possible or outlast a Strength Circuit. The additional benefits would include increasing bone density, preventing injuries, aids in recovery from previous injuries, increases metabolism, improves posture, balance, range of motion, develops a better sleeping pattern, builds confidence and overall a better way to build stamina both physically and mentally.
