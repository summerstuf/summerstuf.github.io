Cardio Boxing:

Focusing on building Cardio Endurance through Boxing. All levels are welcomed.


Strength And Conditioning:

This is an interval training class with a variety of strength stations focusing on building Muscular Endurance and improving your overall strength by giving you a full body workout. All levels are welcomed.


Boxing Technique:

Focusing on the fundamentals of Boxing. Class is mixed with Boxing Stations to help improve Footwork, Head Movement, Cardio Endurance and teaching proper form through focus mitts and bag work. All levels are welcomed.
